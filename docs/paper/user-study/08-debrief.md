# Debrief script

> Read aloud at the end of the session, after the post-task survey is
> complete. The script runs ~150 words at a normal reading pace
> (~60-90 seconds). Pause after reading for participant questions.

---

## Verbatim script

> Thanks for working through those tasks. Before we end, let me tell
> you what we were actually testing and what happens with your data.
>
> The research question is whether a software engineer or ML
> practitioner can use the AMP governance UI for the first time and
> make correct approve / reject decisions without high cognitive
> workload. We're measuring three things: the standard NASA-TLX score
> you just filled out, per-task success and time, and your think-aloud
> commentary about what was clear and what was confusing.
>
> The data goes into Section 5.5 of the AMP paper, which I'm aiming
> to submit to a peer-reviewed venue in the next few months. Your
> participant code — `P##` — will be used in the analysis; your real
> name will not appear anywhere. Quoted excerpts from your think-aloud
> may appear in the paper but will be reviewed first to make sure they
> can't identify you or your employer.
>
> The session recording is encrypted, accessed only by me, and deleted
> within 90 days. The de-identified transcript and the quantitative
> CSV are kept for five years and may be released as supplementary
> material with the paper, so other researchers can re-analyze.
>
> I'll send the USD 15 Amazon gift card to `[email]` within 48 hours.
> If you'd prefer a different brand, let me know now.
>
> If anything comes up later — feedback you wish you'd shared, or
> questions about the study — reply to the recruitment email any
> time. My address is `[PI EMAIL]`. For questions about your rights
> as a participant, the IRB office at `[INSTITUTION]` is
> `[IRB OFFICE EMAIL]`.
>
> Thanks again. This was genuinely helpful.

---

## After the script

*Allow the participant to ask questions. Common questions and stock
answers:*

- **"When will the paper be out?"** Target submission `[MONTH YYYY]`;
  publication typically 6-12 months after submission depending on
  venue.
- **"Can I see my recording?"** Yes — you have a right to access
  your own data. I'll email you a copy if you want one, but please
  treat it as personal to you.
- **"Can I cite the AMP project at work?"** Absolutely — the
  repository is `[GITHUB URL]`, Apache-2.0 licensed for the protocol
  and reference implementation.
- **"How did I do?"** I genuinely cannot tell you in a way that's
  meaningful — the study measures the UI, not you. If you completed
  the tasks, you did fine.

*If the participant pushes for technical / open-ended discussion of
the project, the researcher may stay on the call after the formal
session ends. Make clear the recording has stopped and the post-session
chat is off the record.*
